About Elena

Designing spaces that shape how people feel

Elena Fidalgo is an interior designer with a strong focus on creating spaces that balance functionality, aesthetics, and user experience. The approach centres on thoughtful spatial planning and the development of environments that shape how people feel and interact within a space.

With a particular emphasis on interiors that support human comfort and wellness, every project is approached with a considered perspective — one informed by real-world experience in hospitality and a deep understanding of how design influences everyday life.

Having worked in restaurant environments, this experience has provided valuable insight into the importance of proper workflow, efficiency, and how spatial design can elevate ordinary experiences. This perspective continues to inform the design process, resulting in solutions that are both creative and purposeful.

The work is driven by projects where thoughtful design can transform the atmosphere of a space — creating interiors that feel intentional, welcoming, and quietly refined. Everything is guided by a commitment to developing spaces that enhance the way people experience and connect with their environments.
