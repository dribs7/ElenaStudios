Project 04

The Office

Type

Commercial Workspace

Style

Organic Modern

Scope

Full Interior Design

Focus

Collaboration & Well-being

Materials

Wood, Marble, Natural Stone, Linen

Rooted in organic modern principles, The Office was conceived as a workplace that prioritizes comfort, collaboration, and well-being. A neutral and natural material palette of warm wood tones, soft beiges, and natural stone finishes establishes a welcoming and calming environment throughout the space.

Wood, marble, natural stone, linen, and cotton were selected to bring warmth, texture, and a grounding connection to natural elements. The design moves away from traditional cubicles, instead encouraging interaction through shared work areas and open collaborative zones while maintaining a sense of professionalism and focus.

Expansive windows allow natural light to flood the interior, reinforcing the openness of the space. Recessed lighting, track lighting, and pendant fixtures complement the daylight, ensuring a softer and more comfortable atmosphere during evening hours.
