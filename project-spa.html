Project 03

Luna Wellness Spa

Type

Wellness & Hospitality

Style

Balinese-Tropical

Scope

Full Interior Design

Key Feature

Moon Pool

Materials

Wood, Stone, Ceramic, Natural Fabrics

Drawing from Balinese and tropical design traditions, Luna Wellness Spa emphasizes natural materials and a deep connection to nature. A warm, earthy palette of wood, stone, ceramic, and natural fabrics establishes a calming and immersive environment throughout the space.

The design creates a sensory journey — welcoming and bright at the entrance, gradually transitioning into more intimate, subdued spaces deeper within the spa. Large windows allow natural light to fill the front areas, while interior treatment rooms are deliberately softened and tranquil.

The centrepiece of the spa is the moon pool: a small pool surrounded by natural rock walls where visitors can relax beneath an opening that reveals the night sky. Soft ambient lighting is woven throughout the interior, creating a peaceful and restorative atmosphere that supports total relaxation.
